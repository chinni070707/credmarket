## üìö **COMPETITIVE ANALYSIS**

### **Consumer Marketplaces**
- **Vinted**: Condition badges, buyer protection fee transparency, "closet" concept
- **Mercari**: Simple mobile-first design, escrow payments
- **OfferUp**: Location-based, instant messaging, TruYou verification

### **Professional Platforms**
- **Fishbowl**: Company verification, anonymous posting options, industry bowls
- **Blind**: Work email verification, salary transparency, company reviews
- **Nextdoor**: Hyperlocal focus, neighbor verification, recommendations

### **Key Learnings**
1. **Trust signals are paramount** - Every platform invests heavily in verification
2. **Mobile-first is non-negotiable** - 80%+ traffic comes from mobile
3. **Community features drive engagement** - Forums, groups, following systems
4. **Transparency builds confidence** - Show sold items, view counts, response rates
5. **Gamification increases activity** - Badges, achievements, seller levels
